The IEEE RVR & JC CE Student Branch is dedicated to fostering talent and innovation in the field of industrial electronics. Through our various events and initiatives, we provide valuable opportunities for students to showcase their skills and passion while promoting inclusivity, diversity, and excellence.

Who are we?

IEEE Student Branch STB55201 was founded in 1990 and has since been a
driving force in engineering and technology innovation. Our branch is
committed to fostering a vibrant community of aspiring engineers and
promoting excellence in the field.

Our branch is made possible by the hard work and dedication of our
members. From securing prestigious ambassadorship positions to organizing
successful events, our volunteers have made a significant contribution to our
community.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading hub for innovation and excellence. We strive
to empower students to become future leaders in the field, making significant
contributions to technological advancements and societal progress.

Mission

The mission of the IEEE RVR & JC CE Student Branch is to foster talent and
innovation. We aim to provide a platform for students to showcase their skills,
explore their passion, and contribute to the advancement of technology.

What is IEEE?

A trusted voice in engineering, technology, and research. The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the worldâ€™s largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. The organization comprises 39 technical societies, each dedicated to a scientific or technological field. Collectively, the technical societies make up 30% of all global scientific literature in electrical engineering, computer engineering, and other related technical fields such as biomedical, mechatronics, and computer science. With various societies and chapters in over 160 countries around the world, IEEE spearheads academic research, and sets industry standards.
